Career Counseling High Schoolers During the Age of AI

April 8, 2026

On March 5, Anthropic published some research on the labor market impacts of AI at https://www.anthropic.com/research/labor-market-impacts. The article includes a graph illustrating the theoretical and observed capabilities for a variety of occupations. In the graph, it shows AI doing most of the "whitecollar" jobs. If you had into this graph those occupations that will probably be done by robots in the near future, there isn't much left for humans to do. Maybe there would be occupations for some certain high-dexterity skills and activites involving human-to-human personal care, like nursing. But, that's it.

If that is the approaching future, what do you advise high schoolers to train for, to aim for, in their quickly approaching work careers?
