How We’ll Identify and Deliver Value

AI Studio Methodology


Initial ideas are hypotheses — here’s how we’ll validate and scale what truly helps ParentSquare’s teams.

  • Discover: Partner with Sales, Marketing, and Operations to observe workflows, quantify time sinks, and define success metrics.
  • Design: Co-create rapid prototypes using safe data, LLMs, APIs, and no-code tools to prove feasibility fast.
